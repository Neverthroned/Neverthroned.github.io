About Me

I'm Bridger, I'm a VFX artist who is experienced in game engines such as Unity and Unreal and the associated forms of shader language and tools like shader graphs. I am capable of adding rendering features to the built in URP (Unity Render Pipeline) and solidifying different rendering passes in order to produce stylized, and more general lighting and render effects. I also have experience with tools such as the Adobe suite, Krita, and blender. Given a reasonable amount of time, and an example or artist rendition, I can create a specific effect based in HLSL and C# and bridge the gap between art and the technical limitations of a game engine.

I love every aspect of the game design process, and I just love games in general. Working on a team or even independently to produce something to entertain people really brings me joy. Playing them myself to is just as rewarding, and I love just messing around in engine to find the perfect balance of art and code.
